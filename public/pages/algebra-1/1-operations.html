    • Variables

    Variables are symbols for numbers that we don't know yet. Denoted with letters at the end of the alphabet such as x, y, and z.

    constants are numbers on their own. for example the simple statement 

              
              3 + 2 = 5
    In the above equation 2, 3, and 5 are constants. But what if we replace the 3 with an x? 
              
              x + 2 = 5

    In the above equation x is a varaible.
